Bonan's Concepts and Computation Lab

We are an interdisciplinary lab investigating how the mind works from a computational perspective.

As part of the Institute for Language, Cognition and Computation (ILCC) at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh, we are proudly located at Scotland's beautiful historical center.

The lab is particularly excited by topics including:

  • How do people create new concepts?
  • How do social groups solve search problems?
  • What is the role of language in thinking?
  • What does it mean to create human-like intelligence?

We examine questions inspired by life and philosophy, build models leveraging maths and algorithms from computer science, and run (online) behavioral experiments following psychological research to contest our models.
