Bonan Zhao

Principle Investigator

I am broadly interested in the computational underspinnings of the human mind. My work has touched on probabilistic models of inductive concept learning (NHB paper), human-like generalization from sparse data (CBB paper), and rational decisions in large combinatorial spaces (cogsci paper). I'd also like to think about language's role in thinking.
